About Ben

For twelve years I've helped people stop performing their lives and start living them.

No scripts, no hustle, no shame — just honest conversations that move you forward. I trained as an ICF coach after my own quiet unravelling taught me that willpower isn't the thing in short supply. Clarity is.

I work with people in the messy middle of a career, a relationship, a reinvention — anyone who senses there's a fuller life waiting on the other side of a few honest choices.
