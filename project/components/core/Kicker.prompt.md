Small uppercase gold eyebrow that labels a section above its heading.

```jsx
<Kicker spark>Life & Growth Coach</Kicker>
<Kicker tone="on-dark">How it works</Kicker>
<Kicker tone="pill">Hi, I'm Ben 👋</Kicker>
```

Tones: `plain` (gold uppercase on light), `on-dark` (soft gold on forest), `pill` (tinted capsule — the friendlier treatment). `spark` prefixes a ✦.
