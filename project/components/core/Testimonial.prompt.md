Client testimonial card with a gold star rating, the quote, and an initials avatar + attribution. Drop several into a grid for a social-proof wall.

```jsx
<Testimonial
  rating={5}
  quote="I came in wanting a new job. I left with a different relationship to my whole life."
  name="Sarah K." role="Product Designer" initials="SK"
/>
```

`tone="dark"` for placement on forest sections. `rating` controls filled stars (1–5).
